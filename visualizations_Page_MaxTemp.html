Max Temp x Latitude

Max Temp as a function of Latitude
Pandas and Seaborn

In efforts to better understand weather patterns across the globe, our humble group of data sleuths took to a popular weather API (OpenWeatherMap API) and decided to plot key weather indicators against Latitide which runs up and down on the globe (0 degree latitude is the equator as a refresher).

Temperature: There appears to be a negative relationship as per the linear regression line. However,
we can see that temperature increases towards the equator. A lowess or non-linear transformation may fit the data more appropriately as it relates to temperature.
